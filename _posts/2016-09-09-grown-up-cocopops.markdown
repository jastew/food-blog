prep: 5 mins
ingredients:
  -
    name: Jumbo oats
    amount: 100g
  -
    name: Puffed Rice
    amount: 20g
  -
    name: Rice Milk
    amount: 300ml
  -
    name: Cacao Powder
    amount: 2tsp
  -
    name: Liquid Sweetener (e.g. Maple Syrup, Data Syrup)
    amount: 15g
instructions:
  - Combine the oats, milk, cacao powder and liquid sweetener and leave to soak overnight in the fridge
  - Add the puffed rice and mix through
  - Enjoy! :)
